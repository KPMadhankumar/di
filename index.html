Marketing Insights & FAQ

How long does it take to see results from SEO? expand_more

Typically, SEO is a long-term game. You can expect to see significant ranking improvements and organic traffic growth within 3 to 6 months, depending on your competition and industry keywords in Chennai and beyond.

What's the difference between Meta Ads and Google Ads? expand_more

Google Ads captures high-intent users searching for specific services, while Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram) allows for granular audience targeting based on interests and demographics to build awareness and desire.

Will I get a dedicated report every month? expand_more

Yes! Transparency is our core value. You will receive a detailed monthly report covering all KPIs including traffic, leads, conversions, and ad spend ROI.
